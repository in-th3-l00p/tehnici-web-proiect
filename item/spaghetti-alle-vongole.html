Spaghetti alle Vongole

A coastal classic featuring fresh clams steamed in white wine, garlic, and parsley, finished with high-quality olive oil and a touch of chili.

55 RON Seafood Special

Dish Story

Our alle Vongole celebrates the flavors of the sea with freshly purged clams cooked just until they open, releasing their natural juices into a delicate white wine sauce.

The sauce is lightly thickened with pasta water and finished with parsley and olive oil for a bright, elegant dish.

Key Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh clams
  • Dry white wine
  • Garlic and chili
  • Extra-virgin olive oil and parsley
Portion size 360 g
Perfect with Crisp white wine
Preparation time 14–18 minutes
