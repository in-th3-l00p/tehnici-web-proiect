Spaghetti Puttanesca

A bold, punchy sauce of tomatoes, olives, capers, anchovies, garlic, and chili, bringing the intense flavors of southern Italy to your plate.

40 RON Signature Sauce

Dish Story

Our Puttanesca sauce layers umami-rich ingredients to create a deeply savory, aromatic dish with just the right amount of heat from chili flakes.

We combine tomatoes, garlic, anchovies, olives, and capers, simmering them together before tossing with spaghetti for a sauce that clings to every bite.

Key Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes and tomato passata
  • Anchovy fillets
  • Kalamata olives and capers
  • Garlic and chili flakes
Portion size 340 g
Perfect with Rustic red wine
Preparation time 12–15 minutes
