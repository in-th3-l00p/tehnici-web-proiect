Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe

A pure expression of Roman cuisine: spaghetti dressed in a creamy emulsion of pecorino romano and black pepper, without cream or butter.

38 RON Roman Classic

Dish Story

Cacio e Pepe is all about balance and technique. We finely grate pecorino romano and combine it with hot pasta water to form a velvety sauce that clings to each strand.

Generous amounts of freshly cracked black pepper add warmth and complexity, making this seemingly simple dish unforgettable.

Key Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino romano DOP
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Sea salt
  • Pasta cooking water
Portion size 320 g
Perfect with Fresh white or sparkling wine
Preparation time 10–12 minutes
