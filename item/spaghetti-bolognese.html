Spaghetti Bolognese

Slow-simmered ragù with minced beef, tomatoes, vegetables, and herbs, served over perfectly al dente spaghetti for a comforting, hearty classic.

42 RON House Favorite

Dish Story

Inspired by the cuisine of Bologna, our Bolognese sauce is cooked low and slow to develop deep layers of flavor. We balance ripe tomatoes, aromatic vegetables, and tender minced beef for a rich, comforting dish.

Finished with a touch of butter and parmesan, this pasta is perfect for those who love hearty, satisfying meals.

Key Ingredients

  • Durum wheat spaghetti
  • Minced beef and veal
  • Tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Onion, carrot, celery
  • Red wine and Italian herbs
Portion size 380 g
Perfect with Medium-bodied red wine
Preparation time 15–18 minutes
