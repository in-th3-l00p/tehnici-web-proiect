About La Mitzanu

La Mitzanu brings the authentic taste of Italy to the heart of Brasov, Romania. Our restaurant was founded with a passion for traditional Italian cuisine, focusing on the art of pasta making.

We source only the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Italy, to ensure every dish meets the highest standards of authentic Italian cooking. Our chefs have trained in Italy and bring years of experience to every plate.

Located in the beautiful historic center of Brasov, La Mitzanu offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy exceptional Italian cuisine in the company of friends and family.
